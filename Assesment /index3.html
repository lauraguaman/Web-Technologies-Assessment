Introduction To Music Theory for Composers

Description: This course provides an introduction to music theory for those interested in composing and understanding music on a deeper level. Topics covered include scales, chords, harmony, rhythm, and notation. It’s perfect for aspiring musicians and composers who want to understand the theoretical foundation behind creating and analyzing music. No prior knowledge of music theory is required.
