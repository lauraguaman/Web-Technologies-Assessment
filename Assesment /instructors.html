Our Staff

At our university, we believe that great education begins with great educators. Our faculty members are highly qualified, passionate about their subjects, and committed to providing a high-quality learning experience for all students. They bring real-world experience, innovation, and enthusiasm to their teaching, shaping the next generation of artists.

Sophia Reyes - Graphic Design

Sophia is known for her innovative approach to graphic design and branding. She has collaborated with major cultural brands. Symbolic Contribution: Organizer of the university's first "Design Hackathon".

Isabella Clarke - Photography

An award-winning photographer, Isabella encourages students to tell stories through photography. Her series "Voices of Change" gained international acclaim.

Olivia Bennett - Art History

Olivia brings passion and academic rigor to the study of art history. She has published several scholarly articles on Renaissance art.

Emma Taylor - UX/UI

Emma blends traditional and digital illustration techniques. Her illustrated books have received awards at literary fairs.

Daniel Carter - Fine Arts

Daniel specializes in contemporary painting. He has led international student exhibitions, and his work has been showcased at the Venice Biennale.

Lucas Martin - Sculpture and 3D Design

Lucas teaches modern sculpture techniques. He has participated in public art projects across London and mentors students on large-scale installations.

Mia Turner - Fashion Design

Mia is a fashion designer with professional experience in Paris and Milan. She helps students build innovative and sustainable collections.

Ethan White - Digital Media

Ethan guides students into the world of digital art and 3D animation. His animated short film has won awards at European festivals.
