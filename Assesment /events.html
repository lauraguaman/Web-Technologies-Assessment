Memories of Our Events

At Solent Art University, we believe in the power of hands-on experience and creativity. These events are designed to foster learning through engagement with real-world applications. By participating in workshops, talks, and exhibitions, our students gain practical skills that enrich their academic journey. Our events also encourage collaboration and the exchange of ideas, preparing students for careers in the dynamic world of art and design. We aim to inspire creativity, promote innovation, and create an inclusive space where students can connect and grow.
