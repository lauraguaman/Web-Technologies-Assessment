What courses does Solent Art offer?

We offer a wide range of art and design courses including fine art, graphic design, photography, and more.

How can I apply?

You can apply through UCAS or directly via our university website.

What are the entry requirements?

Entry requirements vary by course. Generally, we require a portfolio and relevant academic qualifications.

Do you offer scholarships?

Yes, we offer several scholarships for talented students. Visit our scholarship page for more details.

Are there international student programs?

Yes, we welcome students from all over the world and offer support services for international applicants.

Is accommodation available?

Yes, we provide various housing options close to campus.

What facilities are available?

Our campus includes studios, galleries, computer labs, and a library dedicated to the arts.

Can I visit the campus?

Yes, we offer open days and campus tours. Check our website to book your visit.

Are internships available?

Many of our courses include internship opportunities with industry partners.

How do I contact admissions?

You can reach out to our admissions team via phone or email listed below.
